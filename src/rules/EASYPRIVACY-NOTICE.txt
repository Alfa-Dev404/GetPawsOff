MODIFICATIONS
-------------
The converter translates the network-blocking subset of Adblock Plus filter
syntax into MV3 DNR rules and intentionally drops:
  - cosmetic / scriptlet / regex / redirect / csp / removeparam filters
    (not expressible in DNR), and
  - consent-manager CORE assets (a CMP's own bootstrap script, web-worker, or
    IAB TCF Global Vendor List). Blocking those breaks the user's ability to
    reject a banner and causes re-inject/reload loops, which would make pages
    worse — contrary to PawsOff's fail-open principle. CMP analytics endpoints
    remain blocked.

No upstream filter text is copied verbatim into shipped code; only the derived
rule data is bundled, with this attribution.
